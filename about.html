Meet Our Founder

Founder & Head Baker

Kaylyn Nzisa

Age: 23 Nairobi, Kenya Certified Pastry Chef

At just 22 years old, Kaylyn Nzisa has turned her childhood passion for baking into a thriving artisan bakery. What began as small batches of cookies for friends has blossomed into NZISA SWEETMAT - a beloved destination for exquisite pastries and cakes.

Kaylyn's journey started in her grandmother's kitchen, where she learned traditional African baking techniques. She later refined her skills at culinary school, blending European pastry arts with African flavors to create her signature style.

Our Journey

The Beginning

Kaylyn starts baking for friends and family from her small apartment kitchen in Nairobi Kenya

First Commercial Orders

Launches Instagram page and gets first paid orders for birthday cakes

Our Baking Philosophy

The principles that guide everything we create

Local Ingredients

We source 80% of our ingredients from Kenyan farmers and producers to support our community and ensure freshness.

Handcrafted

Every item is made by hand in small batches for quality control and personal attention to detail.

Innovation

We combine traditional techniques with modern flavor combinations to create unique taste experiences.

Community

We mentor young bakers and support local food initiatives to grow the culinary scene together.
